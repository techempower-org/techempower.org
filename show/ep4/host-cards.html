Jeff - J1Energy bills -- CARE / FERA / debt
  • CARE: 20% or more off the PG&E bill -- electric often ~a third off. Permanent, sign up once.
  • Income door: ~$43,000/yr household of 1-2 / $66,000 family of 4. Automatic door: anyone in the home on Medi-Cal, CalFresh, W-I-C, or S-S-I -- no income math. Minutes to enroll, no documents up front.
  • FERA (a little too much for CARE): 18% off electric only, ~$45/mo typical / family of 4 up to ~$82,000. Myth: the 1-2-person exclusion is GONE -- singles + couples qualify.
  • ONE application covers both -- PG&E puts you on whichever fits. 1-877-660-6789 / pge.com
  • Debt forgiveness: on CARE/FERA + 90 days or more behind -- 12 on-time months, each month erases a twelfth -- up to $8,000 gone.
Jeff - J2LIHEAP + free upgrades + fire season
  • LIHEAP: once-a-year payment up to $1,500 + separate crisis help (shut-off notice). Covers propane, wood, oil. Never repaid; renters qualify, even utilities-in-rent.
  • Project GO: 1-888-524-5705, press 2 for Nevada County. First come, first served -- confirm funds. Voicemail takes ~40 calls/day -- call first thing in the morning.
  • ESA free work: insulation, weatherstripping, LEDs. Fridge 15+ yrs = may replace. Furnace/water heater: homeowners, unsafe units, "may" repair/replace. Home 5+ yrs old; same income rules as CARE/FERA; renters w/ landlord's okay. One call to Project GO sorts ESA vs. WAP.
  • Medical Baseline: medical need, NOT income -- more power at the lowest rate + extra shutoff warnings.
  • Free backup battery: don't wait to be called -- 2-1-1 or FREED 530-477-3333. Limited supply, ask early. Generator/battery rebate: up to $500 back (CARE/FERA).
Jeff - J3The car -- smog repair + retirement (BAR)
  • Repair: car is '96 or newer + FAILS smog -- state pays up to $1,450 toward repairs.
  • Income: say "about $36,000" single / "about $74,000" family of 4 (live 2026 table: $35,910 / $74,250 -- never quote older figures).
  • Co-pay: at least 20% of the repair bill, paid at the shop. Work happens at state-certified STAR stations.
  • Retirement -- state BUYS the car: $1,350 if it just FAILED smog, no income test / income-qualified: up to $2,000 (failed smog) or up to $1,500 (passed).
  • Money runs on the state budget year that restarted in July -- as fresh as it gets. Apply: bar.ca.gov/cap
