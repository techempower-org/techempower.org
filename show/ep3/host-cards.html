Jeff - J1CalFresh -- the money
  • Income cap = about TWICE the poverty line, before taxes: ~$2,600/mo single / ~$5,300/mo family of 4
  • Worth: up to $298/mo single / up to $994 family of 4 / minimum $24/mo (1- and 2-person households) -- most folks land in between
  • S-S-I myth: FALSE since June 2019 -- you can get both
  • Seniors 60+: no strict up-front income cap (county looks at the NET number, after key bills) / out-of-pocket medical over $35/mo RAISES the benefit / senior with a permanent disability can sometimes count as their OWN household even living with family -- ask when you apply
Jeff - J2CalFresh -- 2026 changes + apply
  • Work rule since June 1: able-bodied 18-64 -- work / volunteer / train ~20 hrs/wk to keep benefits long-term. Exempt: parents of kids under 14, pregnant, medically unable, caregivers, students, and more -- "apply, the county figures out which rules apply to you"
  • Since April: some lawful immigrants incl. refugees lost FEDERAL CalFresh. State version (CFAP), same amounts, covers only certain groups -- green-card holders in first 5 years, abuse survivors -- NOT most refugees. Food benefits won't hurt a green-card application -- free legal-aid advice first. Food bank never asks about status.
  • Four doors: BenefitsCal.com (any day) / statewide 1-877-847-3663 / county offices: 950 Maidu Ave, Suite 120, Nevada City + 10075 Levon Ave, Truckee (Joseph Center) -- local 530-265-1340 / dial 2-1-1
  • True emergency -- say so: expedited track, benefits within 3 days. And answer the county's renewal mail.
  • Nevada City farmers market: Saturdays, downtown Union Street -- CalFresh card welcome. Ask the market manager's booth if Market Match is running -- when it is, match up to $10 extra/visit for fruits + vegetables
Jeff - J3WIC -- the numbers
  • Who: pregnant / had a baby in the last 6 months / breastfeeding parents / any child under 5 -- dads, grandparents, foster parents can enroll the child
  • Income (yearly, whole household): 1 person ~$29,500 / household of 2 ~$40,000 (single parent + one child = household of TWO) / family of 4 ~$61,000
  • Shortcut: already on Medi-Cal, CalFresh, or CalWORKs = income test ALREADY passed -- bring the Medi-Cal card, it doubles as proof of income
  • Produce money/mo: $26 each kid ages 1-5 / $48 pregnant / $52 breastfeeding -- plus staples (milk, eggs, cereal, beans, baby formula, baby food) + free nutrition and breastfeeding support. Pregnant? Enroll as soon as you know.
  • Sign up = appointment (phone, video, or in person): Grass Valley 530-265-1454 / Truckee 530-582-7814 / myfamily.wic.ca.gov
