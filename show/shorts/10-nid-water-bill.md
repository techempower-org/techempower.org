# Nine Fifty a Month Hiding in Your Water Bill

- **Est. length:** ~55 seconds (≈145 spoken words)
- **Source:** Ep4 — NID audit brief (double-verified 2026-07-01: NID's own LIRA application PDF "fixed monthly discount of $9.50" + 211/iCarol record; phone 530-273-6185 verified on both). NEVER say "one-third off the base rate" — stale 2019 framing that now overpromises. Qualifying programs per the application: Medi-Cal (Families A&B), SSI, CARE, BIA General Assistance; treated-water single-family residential; reapply yearly.
- **Freshness:** Phone-confirm "$9.50, still current?" at 530-273-6185 before posting (one stray secondary source says $9.00). Plain "Medi-Cal" follows the Ep4 script simplification of "Medi-Cal for Families A&B" — worst case is a denied application.

--- SCRIPT ---

There's money hiding in your water bill -- nine dollars
and fifty cents a month.

If N-I-D -- the Nevada Irrigation District -- delivers
your home's drinking water, they run a program called Low
Income Rate Assistance. Qualifying households get a fixed
discount of nine fifty a month, every month. That's more
than a hundred dollars a year -- for one application.

Who qualifies? If anyone in your home is on Medi-Cal --
the health coverage -- S-S-I -- the check for seniors and
people with disabilities -- or that CARE discount on your
P-G-and-E bill, you're in. And yes: signing up for CARE
unlocks this one too.

The account has to be in your name, where you live, and
you reapply once a year.

Call N-I-D in Grass Valley and ask for Low Income Rate
Assistance. Or dial two-one-one. Or ask us -- techempower
dot org.
