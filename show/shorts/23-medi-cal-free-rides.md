# Medi-Cal Will Pay for Your Ride to the Doctor

- **Est. length:** ~50 s (~120 spoken words)
- **Source:** Ep4 (Home + Transportation) fact-check — Medi-Cal non-emergency medical transport; corpus rule `medi-cal-nemt-rides` (Nevada County = Partnership HealthPlan; book 5+ days ahead; gas reimbursement for a driver). Verified-claims only.
- **Freshness:** Re-verify the Partnership number (866-828-2303) and the five-day booking window before recording. Medi-Cal-only benefit; Partnership is Nevada County's Medi-Cal plan. Evergreen otherwise.

--- SCRIPT ---

If you have Medi-Cal and no way to get to a doctor's
appointment -- Medi-Cal will pay for the ride.

It's a free benefit, and almost nobody uses it. Rides to
your medical and dental appointments, at no cost to you.

Two things to know. Book it at least five days ahead. In
Nevada County your Medi-Cal plan is Partnership Health
Plan -- eight-six-six, eight-two-eight, two-three-oh-three.
And if a friend or family member drives you instead,
Medi-Cal can pay them back for the gas.

So if a missed ride has ever meant a missed appointment --
that's solved. You already have this. You just have to ask.

Not sure where to start? Dial two-one-one, or come to
techempower dot org and join our Discord.
