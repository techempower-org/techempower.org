# Turn Eighty, Ride Free for Life — the Golden Ticket

- **Est. length:** ~55 seconds (≈145 spoken words)
- **Source:** Ep4 — transit brief (verified 2026-07-01, verbatim on the live county Fares & Passes page): Golden Ticket = 80+, lifetime, unlimited rides on all regular fixed routes, proof of age only; explicitly NOT valid on Nevada County Now paratransit. Senior 65+ half fare with Discount Fare ID card verified same page.
- **Freshness:** Confirm still listed on nevadacountyca.gov/2259 before posting (Ep4 week-of recheck covers it once).

--- SCRIPT ---

In Nevada County, turn eighty and the bus is free.
For LIFE.

It's called the Golden Ticket -- and it might be my
favorite deal in the county.

Nevada County Connects -- the county bus system, the one
folks knew as Gold Country Stage -- gives riders eighty
and better a LIFETIME pass. Unlimited free rides, on all
the regular routes.

What do you need to qualify? Proof of age. That's it.
Show your ID once, and never pay a bus fare again.

One honest note: it covers the regular bus routes -- not
the door-to-door vans, which are a separate service.

And if eighty is still a ways off: sixty-five and up ride
half fare with a discount card.

Know somebody who just hit the big eight-oh? Send them
this. Then dial two-one-one -- or find us at techempower
dot org.
