--- SCRIPT ---

One signup. Twenty percent or more off your P-G-and-E
bill. Every month.

It's called CARE -- California Alternate Rates for Energy
-- a permanent discount on your power bill. Twenty percent
or more off -- and on the electric side it often runs
around a third off.

Two doors in, and you only need one.

The income door: a household of one or two can make up to
about forty-three thousand a year -- a family of four,
sixty-six thousand.

Or the automatic door: if anyone in your home has
Medi-Cal, the health coverage -- CalFresh, the grocery
money -- W-I-C, the young-family food program -- or S-S-I,
the check for seniors and folks with disabilities -- you
ALREADY qualify.

Enrolling takes minutes, online or by phone, no documents
up front. Search "P-G-and-E CARE" -- or ask us at
techempower dot org, or dial two-one-one.
