--- SCRIPT ---

A YEAR of car insurance for one hundred ninety-nine
dollars. In Nevada County, that's real.

It's California Low Cost Auto -- a state-sponsored
program -- and our county's rate is tied for cheapest
in the entire state. One ninety-nine a year if you've
had your license three years with a good record.
Brand-new drivers pay a bit over three hundred --
still hundreds, not thousands.

To qualify: a valid California license, a car worth
twenty-five thousand or less, and income up to about
thirty-nine thousand for one person -- about
eighty-two thousand for a family of four.

The honest part: it's liability ONLY. It covers the
other person's car and injuries -- not yours -- and
the limits are lower than standard. But you're legal,
you're insured, and you're on the road.

Search "my low cost auto" -- or dial two-one-one, or
ask us at techempower dot org.
