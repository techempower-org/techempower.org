# WIC Isn't Just for Moms

- **Source:** Ep3 — claims `wic-eligibility` + `wic-benefits` (both accurate). --- SCRIPT ---

Grandma raising a grandkid? That kid can get free
groceries every month. W-I-C is NOT just for moms.

W-I-C stands for Women, Infants, and Children -- and the
name fools everybody. It's free monthly money for healthy
groceries, on its own card -- staples like milk, eggs, and
beans, plus produce money for ANY fruits and vegetables.

Here's the part almost nobody knows: dads, grandparents,
foster parents -- if you're raising a child under five,
YOU can enroll that child.

And the income limits are higher than you'd guess. A
single parent and one child count as a household of TWO --
and that limit is about forty thousand dollars a year.

Best shortcut: if you or your child already have Medi-Cal
or CalFresh, you've ALREADY passed the W-I-C income test.

Dial two-one-one to get started -- or ask us at
techempower dot org.
