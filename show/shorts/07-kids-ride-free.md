# Kids Seventeen and Under Ride the Bus Free

- **Est. length:** ~50 seconds (≈130 spoken words)
- **Source:** Ep4 — transit brief (verified 2026-07-01 vs live nevadacountyca.gov Fares & Passes page, browser-UA fetch): youth 0–17 free effective Nov 1, 2025, no card/paperwork; adult fares $1.50 Zone 1 / $3.00 Zone 2; transfers free; Nevada County Connects = former Gold Country Stage (2022 rebrand — lineage verified; per Ep4 fact-check FIX 4, don't call the name "new").
- **Freshness:** Grant-funded fare programs can sunset — confirm youth-free + the fare table are still current on nevadacountyca.gov/2259 before posting (Ep4's week-of-Aug-3 recheck covers this once).

--- SCRIPT ---

In Nevada County, kids and teens ride the bus for FREE.
No pass, no paperwork -- they just get on.

The county buses go by Nevada County Connects -- the
system longtime riders knew as Gold Country Stage.

And since November of twenty twenty-five, anyone seventeen
or UNDER rides completely free. No discount card, no
signup. Kids and teens just board the bus.

Grown-ups, it's still the cheapest ride in town: a dollar
fifty for a local ride, three dollars on the longer
regional routes, and transfers are free.

School, work, the river -- for your kids, the bus is now
the FREE way to get there.

Routes and schedules: dial two-one-one, or ask us at
techempower dot org -- join our Discord, we answer.
