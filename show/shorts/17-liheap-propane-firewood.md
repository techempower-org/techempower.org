--- SCRIPT ---

If you heat with propane -- or a wood stove -- your
heating help covers that too. Most folks think this
program is only for the power company. It's not.

LIHEAP -- the Low Income Home Energy Assistance
Program -- pays up to a thousand dollars a year
toward your energy bills. And here in the foothills,
that includes PROPANE, WOOD, and OIL. It never has to
be paid back. Renters count too -- even if utilities
are folded into your rent. And if you're holding a
shut-off notice, there's separate crisis help.

Locally it's run by Project GO. Call
one-eight-eight-eight, five-two-four,
five-seven-zero-five -- and press two for Nevada
County. Their voicemail only takes about forty calls a
day, so call FIRST thing in the morning. The money is
first come, first served.

Or dial two-one-one -- or techempower dot org.
