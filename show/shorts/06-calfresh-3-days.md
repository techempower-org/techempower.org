Almost no money -- and no food in the house? CalFresh has
an emergency track that can load grocery money within
THREE days.

CalFresh is California's grocery money -- what folks used
to call food stamps. You get a card that works like a
debit card, it reloads every month, and it works at the
stores where you already shop.

Normally an application takes time to process. But if
you're in a true emergency -- almost nothing in the bank
and nothing to eat -- SAY SO when you apply. That's what
turns on the expedited track, and benefits can land within
three days.

Apply online, any day of the year, at BenefitsCal dot com.
Or dial two-one-one and a real person walks you through
it. Questions? techempower dot org -- join our Discord.
We answer.
