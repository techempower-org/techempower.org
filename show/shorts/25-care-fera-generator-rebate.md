--- SCRIPT ---

If you live in fire country and the power goes out a lot,
PG&E has a rebate to help you buy a backup -- and if you're
on their CARE or FERA discount, it's worth even more to you.

It's up to five hundred dollars back on a portable
generator or a backup battery -- a base rebate for any
qualifying customer, and a bigger one if you're on CARE or
FERA.

Two catches, both easy. Your home has to be in a
high-fire-risk or frequent-outage area -- and out here,
most of the west county is. And you buy the equipment
first, then send in for the rebate.

One important thing: this program is short-lived -- it ends
December thirty-first, twenty twenty-six, and money like
this can run out before the deadline. So check that it's
still open and still funded, on pge.com, before you buy.
