--- SCRIPT ---

Your car FAILED the smog check. Don't panic -- the
state of California can pay up to fourteen hundred
fifty dollars of the repair.

It's the Bureau of Automotive Repair's Consumer
Assistance Program. If the car's a ninety-six or newer
and it failed its smog inspection, and your income is
under about thirty-six thousand for one person --
about seventy-four thousand for a family of four --
you can apply.

Straight talk: you cover a co-pay, at least twenty
percent of the bill. And the work happens at a
state-certified smog shop -- they're called STAR
stations.

Fourteen fifty toward the repair, for the cost of an
application. Apply at bar dot C-A dot gov, slash cap.
Or dial two-one-one -- or ask us at techempower dot
org.
