# Sixty Billion Dollars Goes Unclaimed Every Year

- **Est. length:** ~50 seconds (≈140 spoken words)
- **Source:** Ep1 — claim `no-fixed-pot` (verified final statement: ~$60B+/yr unclaimed nationally; ~1 in 5 eligible Californians not on CalFresh; ~1 in 5 eligible workers miss the Earned Income Tax Credit; big-four programs are open-ended entitlements). Fact-checked 2026-06-11.
- **Freshness:** Participation stats are from FY2022 federal data — durable framing, but re-check annually. The "funding grows" entitlement claim was re-verified June 2026 (OBBBA cut eligibility but did not cap SNAP/Medicaid/EITC/SSI); re-verify if federal law changes.

--- SCRIPT ---

Sixty BILLION dollars in benefits goes unclaimed in this
country. Every single year.

Not stolen. Not wasted. Unclaimed -- because eligible
people never apply.

Right here in California, about one in five people who
qualify for CalFresh -- that's the state's grocery money
program -- aren't getting it. And about one in five
workers who've earned the Earned Income Tax Credit --
extra cash back at tax time for people who work -- never
claim it.

That's your own money, left on the table.

And no -- you're not taking it from somebody who needs it
more. For the big programs, the funding GROWS to cover
every eligible person who signs up. That's how the law is
written.

So go get what you've earned. Dial two-one-one, go to
findhelp dot org, or visit techempower dot org and join
our Discord.
