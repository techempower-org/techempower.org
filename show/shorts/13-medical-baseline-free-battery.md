If anyone in your home relies on a medical device that
needs electricity -- an oxygen machine, a C-PAP, a
powered wheelchair -- P-G-and-E has a program for you.

the lowest rate on your bill, every month. And when
fire weather brings a safety shutoff, you get extra
warnings -- calls, texts, even a knock at the door.

Signing up is one form your doctor or nurse
practitioner signs.

And the part almost nobody knows: some Medical
Baseline households qualify for a FREE backup battery.
Don't wait to be contacted -- supplies are limited.
Call two-one-one, or FREED, the independent-living
nonprofit in Grass Valley: five-three-zero,
four-seven-seven, three-three-three-three.
