## 2 · Nextdoor (hyperlocal, neighbor voice — no hype)

### EN

Neighbors — a family of four here in Nevada County can earn $66,000 a year
and still qualify for real help: grocery money, health coverage, discounts on
the PG&E bill, free phone service. For some housing programs the line is over
$100,000. Most folks never check because they assume they make too much.

Our small Grass Valley nonprofit built a free 2-minute way to check:
**techempower.org/qualify**. It's anonymous — your answers never leave your
phone, nothing is stored — and it works in English and Spanish. It compares
your household against 33 programs, and every number shows the official
source it came from and when we last verified it.

No sign-up, nothing to buy, no catch. If it helps your household or someone
you know, that's the whole point. And 2-1-1 is always there too — a real
person, free, 24/7.
