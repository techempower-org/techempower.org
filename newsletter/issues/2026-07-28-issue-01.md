Hi — this is the first-ever TechEmpower newsletter, so let's start with the
deal: a few times a year, you'll get one short email when we publish
something new or an assistance program changes. No spam, no fundraising
pitches, one-click unsubscribe at the bottom. That's the whole arrangement.

Since it's our first issue, here's the tour of everything we make — all of
it free.

## The website: [techempower.org](https://techempower.org)

Built around one idea: you (or someone you love) probably qualify for help
you're not using. Start with our step-by-step guides — free and low-cost
internet, free cell service, checking your EBT balance, password managers,
and more. Written in plain language, no jargon.

## "Wait, do I qualify?" — find out in 2 minutes

Our free screener checks your household against **67 assistance programs**:
food, internet, utilities, health care, and more. No account, no phone
number — your answers never leave your browser. En español también.

→ **[techempower.org/qualify](https://techempower.org/qualify)**

## Nearly 150 resources, one searchable place

Food distributions, housing help, utility discounts, transportation, legal
aid — organized and searchable at
**[techempower.org/resources](https://techempower.org/resources)**. Focused
on Nevada County, useful well beyond it.

**Know something we missed?** If a program or group helped you, tell us at
**[techempower.org/submit](https://techempower.org/submit)** and we'll check
it out and add it for the next person.

## Two free apps we made

- **[Candela](https://techempower.org/candela)** — an Android app that reads
  every TechEmpower guide out loud, even with no internet. Built for low
  vision, limited literacy, or listening while you drive.
- **[Forage for All](https://techempower-org.github.io/forageforall/)** — a
  community map of fruit trees, berries, and edible plants on public land.
  Drop a pin, share what's ripe, eat free from your own neighborhood. Web,
  iOS, and Android.

Both are open-source: no ads, no tracking.

## From the workshop

Every issue, this corner features one free, open-source thing we've built.
First up: **[Star Charts](https://stars.realm.watch)** — a shared night-sky
tracker for families. Give each other stars for meaningful acts — kindness,
courage, a hard chore conquered — and watch your family's constellation
grow, night by night. Works on any phone or computer, nothing to install.

→ **[stars.realm.watch](https://stars.realm.watch)**

## We made a TV show!

**"Wait, I Qualify?!"** is our video series with Nevada County Media — real
talk about the benefits most people don't realize they qualify for. Episode 1
busts the biggest myths about who qualifies:

[![Watch Episode 1 of "Wait, I Qualify?!" — busting the myths about who qualifies](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/6hHfE7GkZlg/maxresdefault.jpg)](https://techempower.org/show)

**▶ [Watch Episode 1 free at techempower.org/show](https://techempower.org/show)**

The rest of the season lands there all summer: **Getting Connected**
(internet + phone), **Food** (CalFresh, WIC, the food bank), **Home &
Transportation**, and **Nonprofits & Farmers Markets** — plus a rural
internet special this fall.

## Come hang out

Questions about any of this? Ask a real human on
**[our Discord](https://discord.gg/7wDhAG3vYS)** — no dumb questions. And
the two numbers worth saving: dial **211** for Nevada County's resource
line, or search **[findhelp.org](https://findhelp.org)** by ZIP code.

Thanks for being one of our very first subscribers.
